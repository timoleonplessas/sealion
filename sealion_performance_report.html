Sealion Performance Report

In the “Sealion Performance Report” we combine vessel drawings and technical files to create an one-off (one report for the whole life cycle of the vessel), ship-specific report.

It includes:

  • Tailor-made fouling diagrams based on each vessel’s unique characteristics which allow instant initial estimation of vessel performance during an in-water survey based only on the observed condition of the hull

  • Detailed performance profile tables (Speed/Power/RPM/Consumption) for different loading conditions, weather conditions and fouling types/percentage.

Benefits:

  • Speed/Power/RPM/Consumption tables for different loading, weather & fouling condition.

  • One-off: One report for the whole life cycle.

  • Low-cost fouling monitoring method.

  • Minimize fuel consumption and relevant emissions with proper hull cleaning & propeller polishing scheduling.

  • Direct Estimation. No online platforms and complicated software.

  • Quick and easy to use. Provides instant initial vessel performance assessment.

The result is a unique tool that utilizes hull observations to have in hand a high quality and complete initial performance prediction for your vessel based only on observed hull condition.
